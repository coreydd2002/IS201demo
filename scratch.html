Exploring Music


Ways to Improve Your Music Tastes

  1. Use music platform's special algorithms to discover new music
    • Spotify
    • Apple Music
    • Pandora
    • Youtube Music
  2. If a song ever catches your interest, look it up! (use Shazam or SoundHound or even ask Siri!)
  3. If you discover that you like more than one song by an artist, dive deeper into their discography
  4. Get to know your artists by reading their biographies and watching interviews, you may find a greater appreciation for their music
  5. Attend live shows, concerts, and festivals to experience the music in a new way
  6. Be open minded towards other people's music tastes
  7. listen to TAME IMPALA!
